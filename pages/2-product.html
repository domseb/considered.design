Start teaching quickly and easily

Once your lesson is ready to go live, deploy it in one of the following ways:

  • Into your LMS – students will get a single sign on experience, and grades will be tracked in the LMS gradebook
  • Directly from Smart Sparrow – you can manage enrolments and invite your students to your lesson without the need for an LMS
  • As a public lesson – allow anyone with the lesson’s URL to access it. You can even embed the lesson in any web page (like embedding a YouTube video)

Integrate with your Learning Management System

  • Easily add Smart Sparrow lessons to your LMS course pages.
  • Students experience single sign-on, meaning they only log in once.
  • Grades are automatically reported back to the LMS gradebook.
  • Teachers experience single sign-on to directly access the Smart Sparrow workspace and lesson authoring tool via the LMS.
  • Uses global standards for integration (Learning Tools Interoperability, or LTI).

Knowledge Analytics

Learn what your students know. Without understanding how students interact with content, online learning is a black box. Smart Sparrow’s Knowledge AnalyticsTM gives teachers insight into their students’ minds: what they’re learning and how, what’s not working and why. Access key data effortlessly using our drill-down dashboard to pinpoint problem areas and difficult concepts. These insights empower teachers to continuously improve their teaching approach by adapting to your students learning needs.
