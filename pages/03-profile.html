I’m obsessed with how my process can lead to more considered outcomes.

My name is Dominic, and I’m currently Head of Design for Smart Sparrow in Sydney. Leading design on our product, brand and marketing, over the past few years I've committed to building a design culture within the company. Before Smart Sparrow, I was working on freelance UX projects in Rio and London, and before that I was Head of Design for Augmented Reality specialists Digicave in Brighton (RIP).

Outside of design, film and cinematography are my main passions, and are something I’d love to get more involved with this year. If you have a interesting project in mind and want to collaborate, email me or reach out on Twitter - I'd love to hear from you.
